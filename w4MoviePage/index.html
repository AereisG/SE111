Kung Fu Panda, is an animated, comedy, and action, movie about Po the panda, following the previous movie Kung Fu Panda, Po has intergrated within the Furious Five and serves his duty as The Dragon Warrior protecting the people of China. However, as Shen, the new villian arrives, Po struggles to fight at his best, due to the peacock being responsible for murdering his entire race. Armed with the first cannon, Shen aims to eliminate Kung-Fu and rule over China. In order to stop him Po must come to terms with his past and find inner peace.
